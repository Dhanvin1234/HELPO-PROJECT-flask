Helpo's Values

Helpo’s core values unite students of iqs, teams and levels. They are our shared

understanding of what we do in Helpo – how we make decisions, how we collaborate, how

we treat each other and how we think about the value we are creating for our community of

students, parents and teachers



STAY
HELPING

Always Help, and you be helped

  1. We approach every question with a sense of
    wonder, and we do it with humility and respect
  2. We talk about what went wrong and freely share
    lessons learned with others
  3. We embrace the unexpected and look for ways
    to see change as opportunity
  4. We challenge the status quo and always seek to
    better ourselves, our work and our team
